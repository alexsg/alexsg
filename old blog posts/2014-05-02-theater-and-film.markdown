##Normal

*Normal* is a one-act thriller co-written by me and Cara Brave in late-2010. We were accepted into the One Acts Showcase at Westview High School, and our play was performed in March 2011.

##Hugh and Me

*Hugh and Me* is a short film created in Fall 2011 for a student-led short film class at UC Berkeley. It is the story of a man named Hugh who doesn't know the kind of person he wants to be, until he meets a woman who does.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/hlGVZhTH8NQ?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

**Role:** Script Writer, Cinematographer, Location Scout

##We'll Be In Touch

*We'll Be In Touch* is a cybercrime short film created for Campus Movie Fest 2012, a university-wide challenge to write, shoot, and edit a five-minute short film in just one week. We collaborated with a composer named Brian Cho on the film's score.
